Sikhism Festivals - 2025

Vaisakhi

This event transformed Sikhism into a distinct and organized faith.

View History

Guru Nanak Gurpurab

Celebrates the birth anniversary of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the founder of Sikhism.

View History

Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti

Celebrates the birth of the tenth Sikh Guru, Guru Gobind Singh Ji.

View History

Hola Mohalla

A festival showcasing Sikh martial arts, bravery, and devotion. It takes place the day after Holi.

View History

Bandi Chhor Divas

Coincides with Diwali; commemorates the release of Guru Hargobind Ji and 52 kings from Gwalior Fort.

View History

Guru Arjan Dev Ji Shaheedi Diwas

Observes the martyrdom of the fifth Guru, Guru Arjan Dev Ji, who was tortured to death for his faith.

View History

Guru Ram Das Jayanti

Celebrates the birth of the 4th Guru, Guru Ram Das Ji, the founder of Amritsar and Golden Temple.

Maghi
