CELEBRETING MUSLIM FESTIVALS

Eid is one of the most important Islamic festivals celebrated around the world. There are two types of Eid. Eid al-Fitr is the end of Ramadan. Eid al-Adha is the Feast of Sacrifice to commemorate the sacrifice Prophet Abraham made.

MUSLIM FESTIVAL 2025

EID UL FITAR

Eid-ul-Fitr is celebrated at the end of the holy month of Ramadan, after a month of fasting.On both Eids, Muslims offer special prayers, wear new clothes, and greet each other with "Eid Mubarak".

View History

EID UL ADHA

Eid-ul-Adha, also known as the Festival of Sacrifice, honors the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim to sacrifice his son for God.These festivals teach the values of charity, sacrifice, unity, and gratitude.
